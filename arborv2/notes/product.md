# Product Surface

## Launch Readiness
Release only when search, impact analysis, and pathfinding feel fast, clear, and trustworthy. The gate is tied to [[Startup Grade Lattice]] and the discipline from [[Daily Reflection]].

## Founder Dashboard
Operators need a living cockpit for stats, leverage nodes, and causal routes. The dashboard should express [[Deterministic Context]] visually instead of hiding it behind boilerplate.
